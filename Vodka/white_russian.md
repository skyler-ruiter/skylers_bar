### Ingredients:

* 2oz Vodka
* 1oz Kahlua
* 1oz Heavy Cream
* *(Garnish)* Nothing

---

### Directions:
1. Pour vodka and kahlua into a rocks glass with a big ice cube
2. Top with heavy cream
